## Pain Signals

Real pain:
- AI broke something
- team needs review controls
- wants CI/GitHub integration
- asks for demo
- asks if it can work on their stack
- asks about pricing or pilot

Weak signal:
- likes
- generic praise
- "cool idea"
- no workflow pain

## Rule

Do not build more features until first feedback is reviewed.
